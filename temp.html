Summary: Latitude vs. X

It’s easy to see through this graph there is an intuitive relationship between Latitude and temperature. Temperatures typically rise when the latitude approaches the equator, and this makes sense given earth’s orbital patterns around the sun. However, it’s interesting to visualize the effects the time of year has on this chart. Since this dataset was collected in August of 2018, the northern cities are hotter than usual. This causes the hottest cities to be located from -10 through 40 degrees latitude.
